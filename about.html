Meet Coach Tim

Timnastics is owned and operated by Tim Rizzon, a professional gymnastics coach. He has worked for over 12 years with gymnastics, cheer tumbling, dance and martial arts. This has allowed him to produce multiple state champions, state records and international competitors due to his knowledge and expertise of these disciplines. He is the head Optional level coach at DC Gymnastics and is on the board of directors for the Jersey Optional Gymnastics Association. His guidance has produced 9 collegiate gymnasts and multiple collegiate cheerleaders. He headed the gymnastics program at Immaculata High School for 3 years. He also continues training with USAG workshops to stay with the latest trends. He is current with USAG SafeSport and Professional member status. His goal is to provide the best and safest possible training for: Gymnastics, Cheerleading, Dance, Martial Arts and Coaches alike to ensure proper progression. He holds no bias towards anyone and will strive to instill his knowledge to all students and coaches alike.
